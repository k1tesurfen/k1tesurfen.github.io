Stuff I used to take these pictures

I try a lot and hope it turns out okay. But what I really enjoy are prime lenses. The fact, that the focal length is fix on these, makes the process of taking pictures less quick and dirty. You really need to think about the shot, or at least try a bunch out. But the most important thing is going outside and searching for interesting subjects. The best camera is useless when your subject is not worth taking a picture.
