Lionman: Interactive media table

As a project of the Visual Computing Group at Ulm University we (team of five students) created a interactive media table for the Museum Ulm. At this media station the visitors can discover the 40.000 year old statuette of the so called Lionman in 3D. Aditionally short documentaries and the three dimensional terrain of the excavation site explain the history and discovery of the Lionman.

ReGraT

ReGraT is short for Redirected Grabbing and Touching, a VR project created at Ulm University to learn and explore User-Centered Design for Interactive Systems. The project enables users to experience real touch feedback while grabbing various three dimensional object in VR. The project utilizes hand motion tracking and a 3D printed controller prototype to implement grabbing objects in virtual environments.
